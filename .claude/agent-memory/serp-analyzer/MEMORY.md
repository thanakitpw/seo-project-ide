# Persistent Agent Memory — SERP Analyzer

## Project Context
- Client site: bestsolutionscorp.com (Digital Marketing Agency, Thailand)
- Primary market: Thai SME audience
- Target search engine: Google.co.th (Thai language)
- Content cluster focus: AI & Automation

## Thai SERP Patterns (Confirmed)

### "ai automation คืออะไร" Keyword Cluster
- Intent: Mixed Informational + Commercial Investigation (SME decision-maker audience)
- Difficulty: Moderate — dominated by mid-authority Thai blogs and training sites, no global giants entrenched
- Top domains: kspasiafin.com, 9experttraining.com, stepstraining.co, lenovo.com/th, 1moby.com, spu.ac.th, hocco.co, lffintech.co.th
- Average competitor word count: 1,800–2,500 words
- SERP features: AI Overview (high probability), PAA box, Related Searches — no Local Pack, no Shopping
- Featured Snippet: Likely paragraph type — definition-focused

### Thai AI/Automation SERP Observations
- Competitors rarely cover: ROI calculator, SME-specific cost breakdown, case studies with Thai business names, comparison tables (AI Automation vs RPA vs Workflow Automation side-by-side)
- FAQ schema is used by top result (kspasiafin.com) — a ranking signal to replicate
- Content gap: No pillar page targeting SME angle with service CTA + educational depth combined
- Tools most mentioned: n8n, Make.com, Zapier, UiPath, Power Automate
- LSI terms: workflow automation, RPA, intelligent automation, no-code automation, agentic AI, digital transformation

### Domain Authority Signals (Thai Market)
- 9experttraining.com: High authority in Thai IT/tech training niche — hard to outrank on generic terms
- lenovo.com/th: Brand authority but thin content (product page, not editorial) — vulnerable to displacement
- spu.ac.th: Academic domain — high trust but low commercial relevance
- kspasiafin.com: Mid-authority, good FAQ schema, tool-focused angle
- stepstraining.co: Problem-first storytelling angle, strong CTA — closest competitor model to bestsolutionscorp.com

## Keyword Patterns (Thai Market)
- "X คืออะไร" queries = Informational intent dominant, but SME-focused variants carry Commercial Investigation signal
- These queries reliably trigger PAA boxes on Google.co.th
- FAQ schema significantly increases PAA and Featured Snippet chances in Thai SERPs

## Content Format Winners (Thai AI/Tech SERPs)
- Long-form guides (2,500–4,000 words) outperform short articles
- Problem-first narrative + definition + use case + tool list + implementation steps = winning structure
- Comparison tables (e.g., AI Automation vs RPA vs Workflow Automation) are underused by competitors
- Thai-language ROI examples and local business case studies are a consistent gap
