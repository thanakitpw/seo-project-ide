## Key Patterns

### Thai Content Character Counting
- Thai characters are wider than Latin — for Meta Titles aim for 58 chars max (not 60) to avoid pixel-width truncation in SERP
- Always recommend testing in a SERP simulator before publish
- Meta Description: 155 char limit applies to both Thai and Latin content

### Word Count Benchmarks (Thai content)
- Pillar Page (Mixed Intent, multiple Content Gaps): 3,500–4,500 words
- Supporting Cluster Article (single topic): 1,500–2,500 words
- FAQ / Definition page (informational only): 800–1,500 words

### SERP Feature Targeting Rules
- Featured Snippet: definition paragraph must be 40–60 words, self-contained, placed at top of first H2
- PAA Box: each targeted H3 must open with 2–3 sentence self-contained answer BEFORE elaboration
- FAQ Schema: always implement as JSON-LD in <head>, not Microdata inline; minimum 5 Q&A for this client

### CTA Strategy by Buyer Stage
- Awareness/Consideration (typical for "คืออะไร" keywords): "ปรึกษาผู้เชี่ยวชาญฟรี" — not hard sell
- Decision stage (comparison/pricing keywords): "ขอใบเสนอราคา" or "นัดหารือ"
- Best performing placements: mid-article (after cost/tool section) + end-of-article full-width button
- Secondary CTA for lead capture: downloadable checklist/guide — place after how-to/roadmap sections

### Content Gap Strategy
- Cost/ROI data for Thai SME is consistently absent from competitor content — always include when relevant
- "AI replacing employees" is avoided by competitors — addressing it directly builds trust and differentiates
- Forward-looking sections (Agentic AI, trends) add freshness signal and reduce content decay rate

### Internal Linking for Pillar Pages
- Pillar Page must be linked FROM every cluster article in its topic cluster
- Pillar Page links OUT to cluster articles (supporting pages), not the reverse only
- Always flag missing cluster articles as content opportunities in Strategist Notes
